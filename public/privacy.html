Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ashwin Azer ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit ashwinazer.rocks (the "Website").

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Newsletter: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the Website functions properly. Third-party embeds (YouTube, Spotify) may set their own cookies according to their privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
